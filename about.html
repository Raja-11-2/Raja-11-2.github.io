About Raja Deepu Sooriya Pvt. Ltd.

Founded by Raja with co-founders Deepu & Sooriya, we believe travel should be easy, transparent and deeply personal.

Our Story

Raja noticed travelers — families, friends, solo — struggling with planning, hidden costs and lack of guidance. He teamed with Deepu and Sooriya to build a single platform that does it all: plan, book, guide and support.

Mission

“Not Just a Travel App. Your Personal AI Guide for Every Journey.” — Our AI supports travelers from the first click to the last step.

Vision

To build a next-generation global travel ecosystem where every journey is personalized, transparent, and meaningful. Starting from India, we’ll expand globally.

5-Year Goal

Make MyTripRaja a trusted name across India — the go-to platform for guided, safe and personalized travel.
